    • JAMBO CARE

    JAMBO CARE is designed specifically for primary healthcare settings, where communication between healthcare professionals and patients is critical. The app provides accurate translation of medical terms and descriptions, support for multiple languages, real-time translation, contextualization, and cultural competence. It is user-friendly and easy to use, making it accessible to both healthcare professionals and patients.

    ITRANSLATE

    SOLUTION

    Our digital platform allows health workers to communicate with patients without language barriers

    PROBLEM STATEMENT

    Marginalized communities face significant barriers to accessing quality primary healthcare services due to linguistic and cultural differences.

    ADVANTAGE

    The application's impact extends to the visually impaired, who are able to communicate with health workers simply by talking, as their audible requests are converted to a language understandable by the health worker.
